Ich: Whats the time?
  
Yai: What?
  
Ich: How late is it?
  
Yai: Huh?
  
Ich: What time is it?
  
Yai: Aray?
  
Ich: Naliga aray?
  
Yai: _minutenlanges Gelächter_ I understand you, but we Thai say: Wela thawray?
  
Ich: Ok. So: Wela thawray?
  
Yai: We Thai say _murmelt was vor sich hin_ but for you its dänndiehwonn.
